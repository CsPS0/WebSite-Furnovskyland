Cultural Days and Holidays

Explore the rich cultural calendar of Furnovskyland

January

  • Jan. 1. - New Year's Day (holiday, visiting day)
  • Jan. 6. - Epiphany
  • Jan. 7-13. - January Party Week (holiday)

February

  • Movable Holiday - Carnival, Costume Ball (holiday)
  • Feb. 26-28. - February Party Days (holiday)

March

  • Mar. 1. - Spring and St. David's Day (holiday in British-majority areas, visiting day)
  • Mar. 15. - 1848 Revolution Holiday (holiday in Hungarian-majority areas, visiting day)
  • Mar. 17. - St. Patrick's Day
  • Mar. 23. - Day of Flowers (visiting day)

March-April

  • Movable Holiday - Good Friday, Easter Sunday, Easter Monday (holiday, visiting day)

April

  • Apr. 15. - National Federation Day (holiday, visiting day, NATIONAL HOLIDAY)
  • Apr. 23. - St. George's Day (holiday in British-majority areas)

May

  • Movable Holiday - Mother's Day (first Sunday of May)
  • May 1. - Labor Day (holiday, visiting day)
  • May 8. - Slovakia's Victory over Fascism Holiday (holiday in Slovak-majority areas, visiting day)
  • May 17-23. - Children's Week (school holiday)
  • May 24-30. - Parents' Week (non-working day, visiting day)
  • May 31. - Day of Trees (visiting day)

May-June

  • Movable Holiday - Pentecost Sunday, Pentecost Monday (holiday, visiting day)

June

  • Jun. 1. - Summer Day, Start of Summer Break (visiting day)
  • Jun. 4. - National Cohesion Day (holiday in Hungarian-majority areas, visiting day)
  • Jun. 15. - Parliament Day (holiday, visiting day, NATIONAL HOLIDAY)
  • Movable Holiday - Father's Day (third Sunday of June)

July

  • Jul. 4. - American Independence Day (holiday in American-majority areas, visiting day)
  • Jul. 5. - St. Cyril and Methodius Day (holiday in Slovak-majority areas)
  • Movable Holiday - Festival Day (last Sunday of July, holiday, visiting day)

August

  • Aug. 5. - Anthem Day (holiday, visiting day, NATIONAL HOLIDAY)
  • Aug. 20. - St. Stephen's Day (holiday in Hungarian-majority areas)
  • Aug. 23. - I. Furnovsky's Day (birthday holiday, visiting day, NATIONAL HOLIDAY)
  • Aug. 29. - Anniversary of the Slovak National Uprising (holiday in Slovak-majority areas, visiting day)

September

  • Sep. 1. - Day of Autumn, School, and the Constitution of the Slovak Republic (holiday in Slovak-majority areas, visiting day)
  • Sep. 15. - Day of Our Lady of Sorrows (holiday in Slovak-majority areas)

October

  • Oct. 3. - German Unity Day (holiday in German-majority areas, visiting day)
  • Oct. 6. - Arad Martyrs' Memorial Day (holiday in Hungarian-majority areas, visiting day)
  • Oct. 23. - Anniversary of the Hungarian 1956 Revolution and Proclamation of the Third Hungarian Republic (holiday in Hungarian-majority areas, birthday holiday, visiting day)
  • Oct. 31. - Halloween and State Foundation Day (holiday, visiting day, NATIONAL HOLIDAY)

November

  • Nov. 1. - All Saints' Day (holiday, visiting day)
  • Nov. 11. - Veterans Day (holiday, visiting day)
  • Nov. 17. - Slovakia's Freedom and Democracy Struggle Day (holiday in Slovak-majority areas, visiting day)
  • Nov.30. - St. Andrew's Day (holiday in British-majority areas)
  • Movable Holiday - Thanksgiving Day (last Thursday of November, holiday in American-majority areas, visiting day)

November-December

  • Movable Holiday - 1st-4th Advent (from the 1st to 4th Sunday after St. Andrew's Day)

December

  • Dec. 1. - Day of Winter (visiting day)
  • Dec. 16. - Birthday Holiday (birthday holiday, visiting day)
  • Dec. 24. - Christmas Eve (visiting day)
  • Dec. 25-26. - Christmas (holiday, visiting day)
  • Dec. 31. - New Year's Eve (visiting day)
